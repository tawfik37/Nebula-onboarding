# Nebula Dynamics: Employee Handbook (2024 Edition)
**Doc ID:** HR-001 | **Last Updated:** Oct 15, 2024 | **Owner:** People Ops

## 1. Welcome to Nebula
Welcome to the team! Nebula Dynamics is a cloud-native SaaS provider focused on AI-driven infrastructure scaling. Our mission is to make the cloud invisible.

## 2. Working at Nebula
### 2.1 Core Hours & Availability
We operate as a remote-first company. However, to ensure synchronous collaboration, we observe **Core Collaboration Hours** from **10:00 AM to 2:00 PM Pacific Time (PST/PDT)**. You are expected to be available on Slack and ready for meetings during this window.

### 2.2 Paid Time Off (PTO)
* **Full-Time Employees:** "Flexible Responsible PTO." We do not cap your vacation days, but we require a minimum of **15 days off per year** to prevent burnout.
* **Consecutive Leave:** Requests for more than 10 consecutive business days require VP-level approval.

### 2.3 Holidays
We observe 12 US federal holidays plus the "Nebula Recharge Week" (the last week of December).

## 3. Compensation & Benefits
### 3.1 Remote Work Stipend
Every full-time employee is eligible for a **Home Office Setup Stipend** of **$1,500 (USD)**.
* **Usage:** Can be used for desks, chairs, monitors, or noise-canceling headphones.
* **Refresh:** This stipend refreshes every 3 years.
* **Process:** Submit receipts via Expensify using the category `Office - Home Stipend`.

### 3.2 Learning & Development
You have an annual budget of **$2,000** for conferences, books, and courses. Subscriptions (e.g., Coursera, O'Reilly) generally fall under this budget.

## 4. Communication Guidelines
* **Slack:** Our primary communication tool. Use threads to keep channels clean.
* **Urgency:**
    * *DM:* "I need this today."
    * *@channel:* "The server is on fire." (Use sparingly).
    * *Email:* "Read this when you have time / Legal record."
